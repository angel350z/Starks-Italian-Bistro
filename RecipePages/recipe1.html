Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Itâ€™s simple yet rich in flavor, giving you a taste of authentic Roman cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the pancetta until crisp.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and immediately toss with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season generously with black pepper and serve.
